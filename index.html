Independent Property Due Diligence

Land risk verification and property investigation in Lucknow. Avoid hidden ownership disputes, registry fraud, and litigation before purchasing property.

The Problem

A large share of civil disputes in India involve land. Buyers often rely on brokers or informal assurances before committing significant money. Problems such as unclear ownership chains, forged power-of-attorney sales, pending litigation, or zoning violations may surface years later.

Our service investigates property records and delivers a structured risk assessment before a transaction is completed.

Services

Ownership Chain Reconstruction Tracing historical ownership transfers to identify breaks or suspicious patterns.
Land Record Verification Cross-checking revenue records with registry documents to confirm current ownership.
Litigation Investigation Searching civil court records for disputes related to the property or owners.
Zoning & Land Use Verification Checking whether the land classification matches the intended use.
Site Verification On-ground inspection with photos to confirm location, boundaries, and access.
Risk Assessment Report Clear written report highlighting potential legal and structural risks.
