What We Do

NordGrid Analytics develops deterministic optimisation models that reduce electricity procurement cost for shift-based industrial facilities operating within Nord Pool markets.

  • Models operational constraints (runtime, shift windows, flexible vs fixed load)
  • Optimises day-ahead scheduling under real market pricing
  • Quantifies baseline vs optimised cost over historical periods
  • No infrastructure integration required

89-Day EE Zone Backtest

Simulated Industrial Unit:

  • Total Load: 5 MW (3 MW flexible)
  • 8-hour consecutive runtime
  • Operational window: 06:00–18:00

Results (89 days, real grid data):

  • Baseline Cost: €589,078.91
  • Optimised Cost: €550,458.67
  • Reduction: 6.6%
  • Total Savings: €38,620.25
